You play this game by spending coins to buy towers. 

Building: 
When buying a tower, it will turn red when in an invalid placing location. To exit building mode, click on the HUD or any other invalid placement spot
Towers:
Clicking on a tower, reveals its range. Clicking again on it, hides this. 
Shields:
Enemies being circled by a fireball are shielded, and the shield needs to be destroyed before they can be hurt.
